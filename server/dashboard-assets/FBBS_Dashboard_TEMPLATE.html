TEY methodology: Tax-equivalent yields shown throughout this dashboard are calculated as Yield-to-Worst (YTW) divided by (1 − marginal tax rate). TEY figures do not take into account TEFRA interest-expense haircuts, the 20% disallowance applicable to C-Corporation bank qualified holdings, or any state/local tax treatment. Bank buyers should consult their own tax and portfolio advisors to determine the after-tax yield applicable to their institution.
