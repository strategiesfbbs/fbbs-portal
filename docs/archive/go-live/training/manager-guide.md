# FBBS Portal — One-Page Manager Guide

*For desk/sales leads. Internal tool — For Institutional Use Only.*

## What's different for you
You do everything a rep does, but you read it **across the whole team**. The portal
doesn't give you a separate "manager screen" — you use the same tabs, filtered to the
team level. (At launch, manager oversight is **by convention**, not a code wall: you
*can* edit anyone's records — do it deliberately.)

## Your weekly rhythm (suggested — ‹CONFIRM›)
| When | What | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily (5 min)** | Glance at Needs Billed + any Priority-1 strategy requests | Strategies → Queue |
| **Weekly** | Full pipeline review by rep; clear stale follow-ups | Strategies → Queue, Saved Views |
| **Weekly** | Confirm Weekly CD Recap looks right | CDs → Weekly CD Recap |
| **Monthly** | Coverage/territory review | Map, Saved Views, Peer Groups |

## The four things to watch

**1. Team pipeline** — *Strategies → Queue*
- Filter by Type and Status. Look for work **stuck** in one status too long, and
  unassigned requests.
- 👁 *What good looks like:* most requests in Open/In Progress have a recent update;
  nothing sitting untouched for weeks.

**2. Overdue follow-ups & stale prospects**
- Reps' overdue next-actions surface in their **Home → My Work**. For the team view,
  use **Saved Views** filtered by status (Prospect) + last-touched.
- 🚩 *Flag:* a Prospect with no note/activity in 30+ days, or an account with an
  overdue next action and no movement.

**3. Needs Billed** — *Strategies → Queue (status = Needs Billed) → Billing queue*
- This is finished analysis waiting to be invoiced. It should **drain**, not pile up.
- 🚩 *Flag:* items sitting in Needs Billed for more than a billing cycle. Confirm the
  billing owner (decision sheet #7) is working it.

**4. Coverage gaps** — *Map + Saved Views*
- Look for thin coverage by territory/status, or Clients with no recent activity.

## Coaching cadence (use the data, don't nag)
- **1:1 prep:** open the rep's My Work + a Saved View of their book before the meeting.
- **Coach the habit:** every meaningful touch ends with a strategy request, a note, or
  a follow-up date. If a rep's banks have no recent activity, that's the conversation —
  not "are you working?" but "let's get your touches logged."
- **Use Needs Billed as a teaching tool:** completed work that never reached Needs
  Billed is revenue left on the table.

## What good looks like (team health, at a glance)
- ✅ Needs Billed is short and moving.
- ✅ Few/no overdue follow-ups older than a week.
- ✅ Each rep's recently-touched list shows daily activity.
- ✅ Prospects have notes that show progression, not silence.
- 🚩 Long-stale Needs Billed, growing overdue lists, or reps with empty activity =
  coaching signal.

## 📷 Screens to show in training
- Strategies → Queue filtered to **Needs Billed** (the billing backlog).
- A Saved View of "my prospects, oldest touch first."
- A bank **activity timeline** that shows a healthy cadence of notes/requests.

## Limits to remember
- Manager oversight is **policy, not enforced** today (role matrix §3.4) — edit others'
  records sparingly and on purpose.
- This is internal-only; the team's pipeline/notes/billing never go to clients
  (see [not-client-facing.md](not-client-facing.md)).
