# FBBS Portal — One-Page Sales Guide

*For Institutional Use Only. Internal tool — not client-facing.*

## What the portal is
Your daily workspace for market intelligence and bank coverage. It replaces the
Salesforce day-to-day: today's offerings, every bank's record, your pipeline, and
your follow-ups — in one place.

## Your day in 6 steps
1. **Home** — start here. "My Work" shows *your* clients, prospects, open requests,
   and overdue follow-ups.
2. **Daily Intelligence** (FBBS) — read the market snapshot and the rule-based trade
   picks built from today's package.
3. **Offerings explorers** — CD / Muni / Agency / Corporate / Treasury / MBS-CMO /
   Structured Notes. Filter, sort, export CSV.
4. **Banks → Bank Tear Sheets** — search by name, city, FDIC cert, or parent. The
   tear sheet is the bank's full record: call-report summary, account status, your
   notes, contacts, product-fit, holdings.
5. **Act** — from the tear sheet, **Open Strategy Request** (Bond Swap, Muni BCIS,
   THO Report, CECL Analysis, Misc). For swaps, use **Strategies → Bond Swap** to
   build the client one-pager.
6. **Record it** — add a **note** and a **follow-up date** so the bank shows up
   right in My Work and your manager's views.

## The one habit that makes this work
> **Every meaningful touch ends with a strategy request, a note, or a follow-up date.**
> That's what keeps your pipeline real and replaces Salesforce task entry.

## Strategy statuses (your shared language)
**Open → In Progress → Completed → Needs Billed** (and then archived).
Setting a request to **Needs Billed** automatically queues it for invoicing so
finished work doesn't fall through the cracks.

## Bond swaps in 30 seconds
**Strategies → Bond Swap** → pick a bank with holdings → review **suggested swaps**
(the desk's maturity-vs-breakeven rule auto-applies) or **build your own** → **Size**
a buy leg to balance cash → **Send** (freezes the one-pager) → **Execute** when done.

## What good looks like
**A good strategy request:**
- ✅ Right **Type** (Bond Swap / Muni BCIS / THO / CECL / Misc) and a **Priority** that
  means something (don't make everything a 1).
- ✅ A **Summary** that a teammate could pick up cold — what the bank needs and why now.
- ✅ Source files attached (the portfolio, the call notes) so the analyst isn't chasing you.
- 🚩 *Weak:* type "Misc", blank summary, no priority — nobody can act on it.

**A good note / follow-up:**
- ✅ Specific and dated: *"6/2 — called CFO re: 18-mo CD funding; wants a proposal by
  Fri. Next action: send swap one-pager 6/4."*
- ✅ Always set the **next action / follow-up date** so it resurfaces in My Work.
- 🚩 *Weak:* "talked to bank" with no next step — it vanishes from your pipeline.

## 📷 Screens to show in training
- **Home → My Work** (where your day starts).
- A **Bank Tear Sheet** with the **Open Strategy Request** button.
- The **Strategies → Queue** with a request moving through the statuses.

## Good to know
- **You can see every bank**, not just yours — My Work is a filter, not a wall.
- **CSV exports** include the package date in the filename.
- **Archive** (Operations) has every prior day's package.
- This is an **internal** tool on the FBBS network. Don't share screens/exports
  outside the firm without compliance sign-off.
